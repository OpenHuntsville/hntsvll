Step #2: Enter Your Profile Information

Enter your name, company, social media accounts, and a short bio. We don't have a way of sending messages on #openHSV, so please provide your social accounts as a way to let others contact you.

After you submit your profile, a moderator will review your profile in an attempt to verify that you live in the Tennessee Valley. This is because we're trying to build a network of local professionals. Once your profile's been reviewed and accepted, we'll list your profile on the site!

Have a question? Don't see your industry category listed? Let us know via the contact form in the lower right.
